AI-Powered Restaurant Booking Automation

A professional business case study presentation for Crown Street Food — streamlining reservations with Vapi, Make.com, Google Calendar, and Gmail.

1. Introduction

Crown Street Food, a local restaurant in Lillington, faced challenges with manual booking management — staff spent significant time handling calls, managing double-bookings, and confirming reservations manually.

This project introduced an AI-powered booking assistant integrating:

Vapi

Voice AI assistant to capture booking requests.

Make.com

Automation and integration platform to orchestrate workflows.

Google Calendar

Single source of truth for reservations and availability.

Gmail

Automatic email confirmations and cancellations.

2. Problem Statement

Manual Calls

Prone to human error and miscommunication.

No Centralized System

Double-bookings were possible, frustrating customers.

Staff Burden

Time-consuming confirmations and cancellations.

Customer Experience

Lack of instant booking confirmation.

3. Solution Overview

Create → AI captures booking request → Webhook → Calendar event created → Confirmation email sent
Read   → AI checks existing bookings → returns availability
Update → AI locates event by Booking ID or name+time → updates → sends confirmation
Delete → AI finds event → deletes → sends cancellation email

6. Business Impact

Error Reduction

Google Calendar as the single source of truth eliminates double-bookings.

Freed staff from manual booking management.

Scalability

Future-ready for SMS/WhatsApp reminders and multi-language support.

7. Future Enhancements

  • SMS reminders via Twilio or Vonage
  • Daily summary email for upcoming reservations
  • Loyalty tracking and offers for frequent diners
  • Multi-language support

8. Key Learnings

  • Router + filters in Make.com are essential for separating CRUD operations.
  • Best practices: use Booking ID vs search for updates/deletions.
  • Document workflows clearly but never expose live webhook URLs publicly.
